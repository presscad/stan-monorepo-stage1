Get Involved with Stan

You may get involved with Stan as a (potential) user, a casual code contributor, or a core developer, as described below.

Stan User's and Developer's Mailing Lists

Because our team is distributed geographically, almost all of our discussion of Stan takes place on our mailing lists.

The place to discuss the use of Stan, post examples or ask questions is on

  • the Stan User's Mailing List.

Most of the actual work of designing, coding, testing and maintaining Stan is discussed on

  • the Stan Development Mailing List.

Contribute Citations

To include a citation to Stan, see

  • the guide to citing Stan.

When your paper, report, web page, etc., is ready to be cited itself, send a note to the user's group. Then we'll put it up on

  • the list of citations of Stan.
